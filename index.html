Our Story

About Akshat Hospital

A decade of healing, rooted in Ayurveda, empowered by modern medicine — serving Gangapur City and beyond.

Our Mission

Akshat Ayurvedic & Multi-Specialty Hospital was founded with a clear mission: to bring world-class healthcare to Gangapur City by combining the profound healing traditions of Ayurveda with evidence-based modern medicine.

Under the leadership of Vaidya J.P. Sharma, with 25 years of Ayurvedic expertise, our hospital has grown from a single-room clinic to a 20+ bed multi-specialty facility with a full diagnostic laboratory and rehabilitation centre.

The Akshat Approach

We believe healing is most effective when it treats the root cause, not just the symptom. Our "Ayurveda First" philosophy means we begin every patient journey with a thorough constitutional assessment before recommending treatment.

Our team of qualified specialists — from Ayurvedic vaidyas to MD physicians, dermatologists to physiotherapists — collaborate on every complex case to provide truly integrated care.
